Setting Up Progams to use SOCKS Proxies

Note you'll lose internet and Malleus access if you do this on your primary browser. It's recommended using a separate browser for this.
Firefox
See instructions here
  1. Go to Settings
  2. Scroll down until you see "Network Settings" and click the "Settings" button in that section.
  3. In the Connections Settings, in "SOCKS Host" put 127.0.0.1 and put the port you configured earlier in the "Port" textbox next to it.
  4. Click "OK"
Chrome/Brave
See instructions here
  1. Open Chrome/Brave with the following option: 
     --proxy-server=socks5:127.0.0.1:<PORT>
  2. (Windows): Copy your shortcut to your browser and edit the shortcut
  3. (Linux): Call from the command line instead of the application menu.
